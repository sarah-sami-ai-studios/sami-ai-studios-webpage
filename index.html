Behind the Scenes

The Creative AI Lab

"Our team at Sami-AI-Studios is an amateur AI short film lab. We specialize in entertainment video series, DIY clips, mental health short films, comedy shorts and whatever may come from our creative minds."

By integrating AI visual generators, audio models, and traditional editing suites, we experiment with modern workflows in digital filmmaking. Every project is a testing ground for character design, narrative pacing, and sound synthesis in the age of generative models.

🤖
Generative Media

Using advanced vision algorithms to render worlds, scenes, and characters from script.

💡
Creative Freedom

Unrestricted experimentation across genres—from psychological dramas to lighthearted parodies.

Current Tech Stack

  • 👁️ Video Models: Luma Dream Machine, Sora, Veo 3
  • 🎨 Visuals: Midjourney v6, Stable Diffusion XL
  • 🔊 Audio & Music: Suno AI, ElevenLabs Voice Synthesis
  • 🎬 Editing: Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve
